About Me

I am a dedicated Computer Instructor from Kathmandu, Nepal, with over two decades of experience helping students build strong foundations in programming and computational thinking. I enjoy breaking down complex concepts into simple, practical lessons that learners of all ages can understand.

Over the years, I have guided countless students through their first lines of code, watched them grow in confidence, and seen many of them pursue successful careers in technology. My teaching style is hands-on, patient, and focused on real-world skills.

I believe technology education should be accessible, engaging, and inspiring. Every class is an opportunity to spark curiosity, encourage creativity, and help students discover what they are truly capable of achieving.
